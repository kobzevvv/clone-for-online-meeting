# Interview Questions & Topics for AI Principal Role

## Opening & Background
- Tell me about yourself — what's your background?
- What are you working on right now?
- How did you get into AI/ML?
- What's the most interesting project you've built?

## AI & Daily Practice
- How do you use AI in your daily work right now?
- Do you use AI coding assistants? Which ones? How has it changed your workflow?
- What's your take on "vibe coding" — using AI agents for development instead of writing everything manually?
- Have you built anything with LLMs? Tell me about the architecture.

## Agent Building & Architecture
- Have you built AI agents? What frameworks or approaches did you use?
- How do you handle context window limitations in production agents?
- What's your approach to agent evaluation — how do you know your agent is working well?
- How would you design a self-correcting agent that improves over time?
- What's your experience with RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)? What worked, what didn't?
- How do you think about agent memory — short-term vs long-term?
- What's your approach to orchestrating multiple agents working together?

## Knowledge Graphs & Data
- What do you know about Knowledge Graphs? Have you built or worked with one?
- How would you approach building a Knowledge Graph from unstructured business data — emails, calls, documents, Jira tickets?
- What's your experience with graph databases (Neo4j, etc.)?
- How do you think about entity resolution — matching the same entity across different data sources?

## Causal Inference & Analytics
- What do you know about causal inference? Bayesian models?
- How would you approach marketing attribution — figuring out which campaigns actually caused conversions?
- What's the difference between correlation and causation in marketing data?
- Have you worked with time-series anomaly detection?

## Business Process Automation
- How would you design an agent that collects client support tickets, analyzes patterns, and surfaces actionable insights?
- What's your approach to automating business processes with AI? Where do you start?
- How do you think about human-in-the-loop vs fully autonomous agents?
- What's the right level of automation — when should an agent act on its own vs ask for confirmation?

## Practical Problem-Solving
- If I gave you a dataset of marketing campaigns with spend and conversion data, how would you find anomalies and propose optimizations?
- How would you build an auto-reply system for customer support that knows when it's confident enough to respond and when to escalate?
- Imagine you need to build an agent that reads all company Slack channels, emails, and Jira tickets to create a weekly executive summary. How would you architect it?

## Culture & Thinking
- What's a controversial opinion you hold that most people in AI/tech disagree with?
- What are you reading right now? What ideas are influencing your thinking?
- How do you learn new things? What's your process?
- What excites you most about where AI is heading in the next 2-3 years?

## Role-Specific
- Why are you interested in a startup vs a big tech company?
- What does "T-shaped" mean to you? Where's your depth and where's your breadth?
- How do you feel about talking to enterprise clients directly?
- What's your experience with pair-programming? How do you like to collaborate?
- What are your salary expectations?
- What timezone are you in? How do you feel about async communication?
