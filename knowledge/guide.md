# Improvado Interview Guide

## About Daniel Kravtsov

Daniel is the CEO & Co-founder of Improvado. Computer Science background, won national and international programming olympiads as a child. Had 4 companies before Improvado: Gambling, Rezoma (knowledge management), RTB Media (real-time bidding). Improvado has been running for 9+ years. Last 3 years Daniel has been hands-on writing code, building agents.

## About Improvado

Improvado is a B-Round company that raised $30M during the war/COVID period. Enterprise clients include T-Mobile, Activision, L'Oreal, ASUS with an average contract of ~$90K/year. The company has ~100 employees.

Originally an ETL platform for marketing analytics with data integration from 500+ sources. Now fully AI-first, building AI agents for marketing automation under the vision "Marketing on Autopilot."

Key technical facts:
- 8th version of AI agent in production
- 100% of company uses AI agents daily — from sales to customer success
- Knowledge Graph: 100% of internal data is tokenized and accessible to agents (calls, docs, emails, Jira, everything)

## The AI Principal Role

This is a unique role — a T-shaped person who can do everything: talk to clients, do marketing, frontend, backend, ML models, R&D. The person will be Daniel's partner in R&D, pair-coding together, building agents, solving client problems.

First 3-6-9 months:
- Month 1-3: Onboarding, understanding the data stack, building first agents, pair-coding with Daniel
- Month 3-6: Own projects — Knowledge Graph improvements, causal inference for marketing, new agent capabilities
- Month 6-9: Leading AI initiatives, talking to clients directly, R&D on new models

## Working at Improvado

- Remote-first but timezone matters (PST preferred, or overlap 5+ hours)
- Relocation assistance available (Argentina is popular — good timezone)
- Fast-paced, startup energy despite 9 years
- Direct access to CEO (Daniel works hands-on daily)
- Equity + competitive salary (open range, candidate-first approach)
